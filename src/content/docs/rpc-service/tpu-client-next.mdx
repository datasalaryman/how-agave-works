---
title: tpu-client-next
---

Agave uses a specialized crate called
[`tpu-client-next`](https://github.com/anza-xyz/agave/tree/master/tpu-client-next)
to send transactions to the TPU.

It is responsible for grouping the transactions into batches, and creating the
worker threads and a scheduler for them that will ultimately send the
transactions to the TPU.

While not directly responsible for getting the leader network addresses, it
enforces a `LeaderUpdater` trait that allows it to call the next leaders to send
transactions to.
